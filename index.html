Work

A collection of some of my thoughout my time at uni including some personal projects for hoing my skills

Trap Hallway

1st year assignment utulising 3d modeling, animations and basic logic. The player must unlock and traverse a hallway housing 3 unique obstilces

Projectors

2nd year assignment attmepting to replicate the realistic projectors from Control(2019), using 2 game engines

The Idol

2nd year project exploring level design theory. The player has limited time to navigate a mystical temple after stealing an artifact to return it to its rightful place in an unknown realm

The Gelatinous Cube
(Update In progress)

2nd year assignment exploring Game play theory & programming. A range of player & envirnmental mechanics are implamented and utulised here including, enemy lock on, swithc controlled platforms and a self replicating enemy A.I

Voxel Destruction

3rd year assignment creating a tech demo of a chosen game mechanic

Tricky Towers De-make

First of three 3rd year group assignments. We were tasked with de-making a contemporary game so that it would run on the Atari. I worked on the main game loop; including the main menu, game scene and end game scene

Mindy Anna jones and the
Technicolour Dream Cavern

Second of three 3rd year group assignments. Similare to the first assignment, we needed to make a vertical slice of a game that could run on limited hardware at 60fps and 1080p. The device in question was single core, and had only 4GB of RAM. I took part in the level design, gameloop and some envirnmental logic

Global Game Jam 2023:
Route to the root (of all evil)

Final 3rd year group assignment was taking part of the 2023 Global Game Jam, 'Roots'. The player has a limited time send adventures into an unchargted dungeon full of creatures, obsticles and traps in order to find the Boss and destroy it. I was responsible for the game loop, the enemy/obsticle encounters and a majority of the games artwork including the encouter tiles, the main menu and the end game scenes

Burglar Battle

This was a large sale assignment that involved all 40+ memebers of the course. This 4 player couch co-op game has 2 teams pitter against each other in a strange mansion, where they must navigate their way to the top floor where a vaul is located. Along the way there are gaurds, traps, puzzles and enticing loot presented to the players. My major role was desinging the second floor of the game, I as also responsible for some of the set dressing and designing of traps.
